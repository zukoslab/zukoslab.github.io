Pocket Mobs

Pocket mobs is a datapack inspired by Pokemon! You can craft Pocketballs that
allow you to capture mobs as an item and place them down in any part of your
world at a later date. Incredibly useful for mob transportation!

The datapack is still being developed as not all mobs NBT data is stored
when captured. So some mobs such as sheep may have different colours than
the one you captured.

As of right now, the Axolotl, Bogged, Bee, and Cat are the only mobs who
store mostly correct NBT, with plans to include every mob at a
later date.
