Minestrike

VALORANT inspired Minecraft Minigame.

Minestrike is a public minigame powered by datapacks, hosted on the
Zuko's Lab server.
In the game there are two teams, the attackers, and the defenders.
The attackers goal is to plant a tnt bomb on one of the bomb sites while the
Defenders need to stop the tnt from exploding.

At the same time, there are a variety of custom coded kits which the players
can select. Each kit has their own theme and custom abilities,
which you can use to help win the game.
